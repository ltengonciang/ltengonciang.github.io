Artificial White Sand
Beach On Manila Bay
The Philippine government's most recent project
which is the artificial white sand beach on Manila
bay amidst a pandemic is just a waste of money
The artificial beach aims for the beautification
of the Manila bay and the government spent a lot
of money for the construction of the project.
And there are a number of more useful things
the government should have focused first like
building more health care facilities and hospitals
for the increasing number of covid-19 positives.
And this website contains a satirical essay about
the issue which criticizes the government for
wasting a lot of money for a thing Filipinos do not
need.
